Upcoming Presentations About the William Blake Archive



Matthew Kirschenbaum and Joseph Viscomi will speak as part of a panel on " Refining Our Notions of What (Digital) Images Really Are" at the 1999 ACH-ALLC International Humanities Computing Conference, June 9-13, University of Virginia, Charlottesville.

Morris Eaves, Robert Essick, and Joseph Viscomi will speak on "Editorial Principles in the William Blake Archive" at the Tenth International Congress on the Enlightenment, University College, Dublin, 25 - 31 July 1999.

Joseph Viscomi will give a plenary lecture entitled "Digital Facsimiles: Reading the William Blake Archive" at Romanticism and the New, the seventh annual conference of the North American Society for the Study of Romanticism to be held August 12-15, 1999 in Halifax, Nova Scotia, Canada.
